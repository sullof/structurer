Structurer

Shape stories with visual narrative boards

A private workspace for story structure

Structurer helps writers build stories with visual boards, color-coded notes, characters, and narrative phases such as Hero's Journey and Three-Act Structure.

Privacy first

Everything you write stays in your browser local storage. No server-side database is used, no user accounts are created, and no cookies are required for app data.

Included demos

The app includes simple demo boards (The Matrix and Jurassic Park) to show the workflow. They are intentionally lightweight examples: your own boards can be far more detailed and complex.
